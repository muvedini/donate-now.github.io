About Us

Donate Now is a not-for-profit organization that focuses on transforming the educational landscape of rural India.The organization creates digital classrooms for children in remote villages, with the support of local communities and Volunteer Teachers from across the globe. With standardized lesson plans and teaching aids for a consistent learning experience, the children from government schools receive e-quality education for a promising & rewarding future.
